Maintainers & Escalation

InfraForge Labs is currently maintained by a single founder maintainer. All releases are built, reviewed, and distributed centrally to ensure consistency and long-term stability.

  • One centrally maintained runtime and release stream
  • No monetization inside execution logic
  • Escalation required for trust-impacting changes
  • Succession rules protect continuity
