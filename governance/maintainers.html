Maintainers & Escalation

InfraForgeLabs is currently stewarded by a single founder maintainer.

Governance rules exist to ensure that future contributors and maintainers cannot weaken core principles under commercial or external pressure.

  • One shared codebase for all users
  • No monetization inside execution logic
  • Mandatory escalation for trust-impacting changes
  • Clear succession rules for continuity
