Maintainers & Escalation

InfraForgeLabs is currently stewarded by a single founder maintainer.

Governance documents exist to ensure that as contributors and maintainers join in the future, core principles remain protected.

Key Rules

  • One codebase for all users
  • No feature gating or paid-only execution paths
  • No license bait-and-switch
  • No forced cloud dependencies

Clear escalation rules ensure that decisions affecting trust or monetization are reviewed carefully and transparently.
