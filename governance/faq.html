Governance FAQ

Clear answers about licensing, sustainability, and how InfraForge Labs operates long term.

← Back to Governance

Is this really free?

Yes. InfraForge Labs tools are free to use for learning and local execution. You can run them offline without subscriptions or cloud dependencies.

Is this open source?

No. The source code is proprietary and not publicly distributed. This allows InfraForge Labs to remain sustainable, consistent, and professionally maintained over the long term.

Why do paid plans exist?

Paid plans support continued development and unlock professional features such as advanced insights, team usage, and extended tooling. Core functionality remains free.

Can I use this commercially?

Yes. Commercial use is allowed under InfraForge Labs license terms. Redistribution, resale, or hosted offerings may require a separate agreement.

What happens if I stop paying?

Nothing breaks. Any locally installed tools continue to work. Paid features simply become unavailable until the license is renewed.
