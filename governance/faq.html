Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. All core functionality is open-source and available to everyone.

Why do paid plans exist?

Paid plans support sustainability, documentation, and support — not feature access.

Can I use this commercially?

Yes. Commercial use is fully allowed under the project license.

What happens if I stop paying?

Nothing breaks. Your tools continue to work exactly as before.
