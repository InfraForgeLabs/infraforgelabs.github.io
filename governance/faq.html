Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. All core functionality is open-source and available to everyone.

Why do paid plans exist?

Paid plans support sustainability, documentation, training, and support. They do not unlock features.

Can I use this commercially?

Yes. Commercial use is allowed under the project license.

What happens if I stop paying?

Nothing breaks. Your tools continue to work as before.

Is this an open-core model?

No. There are no closed or paid-only core components.
