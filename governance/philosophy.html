Project Philosophy

InfraForge Labs exists to build DevOps and infrastructure tools that are free, local-first, and designed to keep engineers in control of their systems.

  • No feature gating based on payment
  • No forced cloud dependency
  • No opaque execution logic
  • No license bait-and-switch

These principles apply to all current and future projects under InfraForge Labs.
