Project Philosophy

Free · Local · Open · Forever

InfraForgeLabs exists to build DevOps and infrastructure tools that remain permanently open, local-first, and controlled by engineers.

  • No feature gating based on payment
  • No license bait-and-switch
  • No forced cloud dependency
  • No hidden execution logic

These principles apply to all current and future projects under InfraForgeLabs.
