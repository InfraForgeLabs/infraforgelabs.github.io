Project Philosophy

Free · Local · Open · Forever

InfraForgeLabs builds infrastructure and learning tools that remain permanently open, local-first, and free from forced cloud dependencies.

  • No feature gating based on payment
  • No license bait-and-switch
  • No hidden execution logic
  • No telemetry without consent

These principles apply to all current and future InfraForgeLabs projects.
