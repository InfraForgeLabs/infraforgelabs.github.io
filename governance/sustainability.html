Sustainability & Funding

InfraForgeLabs projects are free to use for everyone. No payment is required to access features or functionality.

Optional paid offerings exist to support:

  • Long-term maintenance
  • Documentation and education
  • Security reviews and quality assurance
  • Support and response guarantees

Paid offerings do not unlock features or change behavior. All users run the same code.
