Sustainability & Funding

How InfraForge Labs remains independent, sustainable, and user-aligned over the long term.

← Back to Governance

All InfraForge Labs projects provide free, local-first core functionality. Payment is not required to run, learn from, or experiment with the tools.

Optional paid offerings support:

  • Long-term maintenance and stability
  • Documentation and structured education
  • Security reviews and quality assurance
  • Support guarantees for professional use

Paid offerings enable advanced workflows such as team usage, extended insights, and professional support. Core execution remains local-first, predictable, and consistent for all users.
