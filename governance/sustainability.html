Sustainability & Funding

All InfraForge Labs projects provide free, local-first core functionality. Payment is not required to run or learn from the tools.

Optional paid offerings support:

  • Long-term maintenance
  • Documentation & education
  • Security reviews & QA
  • Support guarantees

Paid offerings support professional use cases such as advanced insights, team collaboration, and extended tooling. Core execution remains local-first and consistent for all users.
